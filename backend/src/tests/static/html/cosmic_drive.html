Patch History

V14.19
  • Ability power reduced to 70 from 80.
  • Movement speed reduced to 4% from 5%.
  • Unique Passive - Spelldance:
    • New Effect: Dealing magic or true damage to an enemy champion grants you 20 bonus movement speed for 4 seconds.
    • Old Effect: Dealing ability damage to an enemy champion grants you 40 60 (based on level) bonus movement speed for 2 seconds.
V14.15
  • Health increased to 350 from 250.
  • Spelldance bonus movement speed increased to 40 60 (based on level) from 25 60 (based on level).
V14.3
  • Bug Fix: Spelldance now properly activates upon dealing damage with in-progress persistent damage spells.
V14.2
  • Bug Fix: Now is properly displayed in the health and regeneration section of the shop.
V14.1
  • New:
    • Recipe: Kindlegem + Aether Wisp + Fiendish Codex + 450 = 3000.
    • Stats: 250 health, 80 ability power, 25 ability haste, 5% movement speed.
    • Unique Passive - Spelldance: Dealing ability damage to an enemy champion grants you 25 60 (based on level) bonus movement speed for 2 seconds.
    • Limited to 1 Cosmic Drive.
  • Old:
    • Recipe: Fiendish Codex + Aether Wisp + Amplifying Tome + 815 = 3000.
    • Stats: 100 ability power, 30 ability haste, 5% movement speed.
    • Unique Passive - Spelldance: Damaging an enemy champion with a basic attack or ability damage grants 2.5% bonus movement speed every 1.5 seconds over the next 5 seconds, stacking up to 4 times for a total of 10% and refreshing the duration with each damage instance. At maximum stacks, gain an additional 10% bonus movement speed.
    • Limited to 1 Cosmic Drive.
V13.8
  • Ability power increased to 100 from 90.
  • Unique Passive - Spelldance:
    • New Effect: Damaging a champion generates a stack of 2.5% bonus movement speed every 1.5 seconds for the next 5 seconds, up to 10% at 4 stacks. At maximum stacks, gain an additional 10% bonus movement speed, for a total of 20%. Dealing damage refreshes this effect.
    • Old Effect: After dealing 3 instances of damage from basic attacks or ability damage to champions within 3 seconds, gain 15% bonus movement speed, decaying to 5% over 2 seconds, and 40 ability power for 5 seconds, refreshing on damage dealt to champions though not resetting the effect.
V13.5
  • New Recipe: Fiendish Codex + Aether Wisp + Amplifying Tome + 815 = 3000.
    • Old Recipe: Fiendish Codex + Aether Wisp + Ruby Crystal + 850 = 3000.
  • Ability power increased to 90 from 65.
  • Health reduced to 0 from 200.
V11.24
  • Ability power reduced to 65 from 75.
  • Health reduced to 200 from 250.
  • Spelldance bonus movement speed reduced to 15% from 20%.
    • Spelldance minimum bonus movement speed reduced to 5% from 10%.
V11.23
  • New Recipe: Fiendish Codex + Aether Wisp + Ruby Crystal + 850 = 3000.
    • Old Recipe: Fiendish Codex + Kindlegem + Amplifying Tome + 865 = 3000.
  • Ability power reduced to 75 from 80.
  • Health increased to 250 from 200.
  • Ability haste increased to 30 from 20.
  • Bonus movement speed increased to 5% from 0%.
  • Unique Passive - Spelldance:
    • New Effect: After damaging a champion with 3 separate basic attacks or abilities within 3 seconds, gain 20% bonus movement speed, decaying to 10% over 2 seconds, and 40 ability power until exiting champion combat (5 seconds).
    • Old Effect: If you have at least 160 ability power, gain 20 ability haste and 20 bonus movement speed.
V11.13
  • Ability power increased to 80 from 75.
  • Spelldance bonus movement speed changed to 20 at all levels from 10 30 (based on level).
V11.4
  • Ability haste reduced to 20 from 30.
  • Unique Passive - Spelldance:
    • New Effect: If you have at least 160 ability power, gain 20 ability haste and 10 30 (based on level) bonus movement speed.
    • Old Effect: Dealing ability damage grants 10 (+ 20% ability haste) bonus movement speed for 4 seconds.
V10.24
  • Updated icon.
  • Ability power increased to 75 from 70.
V10.23 - Added
  • Recipe: Fiendish Codex + Kindlegem + Amplifying Tome + 865 = 3000.
  • Stats: 70 ability power, 200 health, 30 ability haste.
  • Unique Passive - Spelldance: Dealing damage with abilities grants 10 (+ 20% ability haste) bonus movement speed for 4 seconds.
  • Limited to 1 Cosmic Drive.
