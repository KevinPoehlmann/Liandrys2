League of Legends V25.06[edit | edit source]

Client[edit | edit source]

Champion Mastery
  • Updated mastery crest icons and SFX.
Champion select
  • Bug Fix: Anonymized player names in a Ranked Solo/Duo lobby are no longer missing from the chat log.
Honor
  • Added a mission to earn up to two Hextech Chest + Key combo rewards while maintaining a positive Honor standing.
    • 1200 mission points required per reward. Players at level 5 earn 10 points per minute played, players at Honor level 4 earn 2 points per minute played, players at Honor level 3 earn 1 point per minute played.
    • Lasts until the end of Welcome to Noxus Act 2.
  • As announced in patch V25.04, the Honor Shop and Honor tokens will be retired during this patch.
    • All Honor skins will be added to the Honor Shop after this patch's launch if players wish to redeem their tokens separately.
    • Unspent Honor 5 Tokens will be automatically converted to 1050 each.
    • Players still keep all their unlocked Honor skins prior to the release of these changes.
  • Bug Fix: Level 5 icon is no longer missing from the Loading screen.
  • Bug Fix: Honor Recall is no longer granted while at level 4 for a single game after simply receiving at least one Honor vote in the previous game.
Metagames
  • New metagame: The Demon's Hand.
Mythic Shop
  • New schedule "categories". The following amount to 84 guaranteed items on top of the current event's which rotate significantly faster.
    1. Featured content: a manual assortment of items that does not have a set expiration time; it instead rotates when the next event starts. This category includes the latest event's rotation like Prestige skin and other top-tier items such as Nexus Finishers.
    2. 2-week rotation of 12 random Seasonal and/or Legacy Mythic skins.
    3. 1-week rotation of 8 random previous Event chromas.
    4. 1-day rotation of 4 random accessories, (Profile Icons, Emotes, Ward skins).
  • Event chroma + profile icon bundles and are now separate items.
    • Event chromas cost 35.
    • Profile icons cost 5.
  • These will go live at 00:00 UTC on March 20th, 2025, after which the new timers will start counting for non-Featured content regardless of patch cycles.
  • Because content is now generated randomly, there is no longer a set schedule that can be communicated in advance.
Sanctum
  • Due to the release of Sahn-Uzal Mordekaiser being delayed, the Quantum Galaxy Slayer Zed banner will remain until the next banner releases (on Live servers).
Summoner's Rift
  • Music
    • New spawn-in theme.
    • Added contextual Black Rose choir voices. Their number increase throughout the game.
Vanguard Ranked Rollbacks
  • In Ranked queues, if a team lost with a cheater who was subsequently detected and suspended, players will now have the LP they lost restored after the punishment has been issued to the cheater.
  • Added new notification reports related to Vanguard actions.
    • For games affected by cheaters in both Ranked and non-Ranked queues, the notification will include the name of the reported cheater who has been suspeended.
    • For games affected by cheaters in Ranked queues, the notification will include the name of the detected cheater who has been suspended (regardless of the player reporting them) along with the date stamp of the game, the LP restored and the player's new League standing after that restoration.
Your Shop
  • Returns between the 25.06 and 25.07 patch cycles.
    • Editor's note: Exact time TBD—in the dev blog, Riot stated that the schedule may no longer align with the time that patch cycles start and end.

  • Bounty suppression activation time reduced to 6:00 from 14:00.
  • Bounty devaluation while at a deficit changed to 30 / 60 / 90 / 100% (based on Objective deficit) from 20 / 40 / 60 / 80% (based on Objective deficit).

  • Bug Fix: Issuing a move command while rooted no longer sometimes accidentally counts as an attack-move command.

  • Bug Fix: Certain buff tooltips no longer display debug text.
Practice Tool
  • Bug Fix: The Target Dummy's damage counter no longer sometimes disagrees with the actual damage dealt and the floating text display.

  • Bug Fix: Feats of Strength now properly reset after resetting the game.

  • Bug Fix: Spawn Jungle Plants no longer stops working after resetting the game.

Riot Vanguard
Riot Vanguard is a ring 0 (kernel) custom anti-cheat security software developed by Riot Games. It is designed to uphold the highest levels of competitive integrity for League of Legends, Teamfight Tactics, 2XKO, and Valorant. The application consists of a kernel-mode driver (vgk.sys) that starts on system boot and passively monitors the system, and a client that actively monitors the system any time Riot Client, League Client, or League of Legends is open. Vanguard is developed with a priority on both game safety and personal computer safety.

Root
A unit that is rooted is unable to control its movement or activate movement spells for the duration. This effect does not innately knockdown a target that is already performing a dash. Rooted units can still be displaced.
Root is an immobilizing effect.

Moving includes movement commands. Movement spells include all dash and blink abilities - including Recall / Empowered Recall and the summoner spells Flash, Teleport, Hexflash, and Dash, plus a number of additional cases.

Interrupts: Interrupts movement channels unless otherwise stated.
Reduction: The duration is affected by Tenacity.
Resist: Resisted by crowd control immunity and Dr. Mundo's Goes Where He Pleases.
Removal: Removed by Cleanses.

  • Crowd control applied by one's self or an ally cannot be countered in any way, neither reduced in duration, resisted, nor removed unless special cased to do so.
    • The crowd control effect may still be cleansed if it is applied as a debuff separate from the originated ability.

Active: Naafiri goes on the hunt for a period, briefly becoming untargetable and vanishing her Packmates, gaining  bonus attack damage and granting herself and her Packmates  bonus move speed. She will also summon extra Packmates that last for the hunt's duration.
Casting Hounds' Pursuit briefly extends The Call of the Pack's hunt duration.
