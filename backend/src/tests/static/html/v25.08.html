League of Legends V25.08[edit | edit source]

Client[edit | edit source]

Ability Videos
  • Bug Fix: Fixed an issue where LeBlanc's Ability Video's didn't reflect her new update.
Blue Essence Emporium
  • The Blue Essence Emporium will be active through 25.08 and will be turning off when 25.09 goes live.
Ranked
  • SR Ranked Season 1 will end April 29, 2025 midnight, regional local time, which is the last day of patch 25.08.

Spell shield

A spell shield refers to one of several effects that have the capacity to negate hostile abilities from applying to the recipient. A spell shield may negate many active effects coming from champion abilities, epic monsters, champion summoned units, and items, but almost none from runes and summoner spells with the only exceptions of Arcane Comet, Unleashed Smite, and Primal Smite which will be blocked.

  • See the article for more details.
